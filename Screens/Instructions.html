INSTRUCTIONS:

The following task has 3 parts. Each one has an initial INDIVIDUAL part and then a GROUP part.

In the INDIVIDUAL part you will listen two times a sentence with noise. After the two times you will have to write the sentence on the computer. You can use the notebook to write notes while listening.

When you have submit the sentence you will have to go to the GROUP computer. There you have to write your answer as a GROUP.

After you have answer the question as a GROUP you will have to come back to your computer to listen the next sentence.
